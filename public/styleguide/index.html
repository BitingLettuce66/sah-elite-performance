SAH Elite Performance · design direction v2

Quiet Luxury, executed.

A chooser, not a verdict. Below are the three accent options and three display-font options from DESIGN-DIRECTION.md, applied to a real component set. Compare them side-by-side, then use the live preview at the bottom to try a combination and pick your favourite. On a phone, a one-tap Accent / Font bar floats at the bottom so you can flip live. Nothing here touches the live app.

★

Recommended to start: Accent A · Bright ice + Geist

Why: this is also what the live app already ships, so choosing it changes nothing and locks in a look that's proven on your phone. Ice-blue A is bright enough to read clearly as the “today / live” signal against the dark cool-neutral background without tipping into neon — that restraint is the whole direction. Geist is the quietest and most precise of the three display fonts (Swiss, software-grade), which keeps the big metrics razor-legible and lets the numbers, not the typeface, do the talking. This page opens on that combo — flip any option to compare; nothing is locked, the final pick is a call we make together.
